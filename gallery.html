Kenya International Industrial Expo(KNCCI)

The Kenya National Chamber of Commerce and Industry is registered as a not-for-profit private company limited by guarantee under the Companies Act Chapter 486 of the laws of Kenya. Before the promulgation of the Kenyan Constitution in August 2010, the KNCCI had its main operations at the head office in Nairobi with regional offices in the major towns in Kenya. However, after the promulgation of the constitution, the Chamber amended the legal instrument to establish a National Office and County Chamber’s offices in all the 47 Counties.

Kenya International Trade Exhibition Africa (KITE)

Kenya International Industrial Expo (KIIE for short) started in 2018, organized by AFRIPEAK EXPO(KENYA) LTD. With the strong support of relevant organizations at home and abroad, and the high recognition of domestic and foreign enterprises, after five years of careful cultivation, the exhibition scale and the number of professional visitors has been increasing year after year. KIIE has become an important way for Chinese enterprises to enter the Kenyan and East African markets, and an important platform for two-way investment and trade exchanges between China and Africa
